Common questions
Are your workshops really free? ▼
Yes — every EEI program is completely free to attend. We are a nonprofit organization funded entirely by donations and grants.
Can you come to our school or workplace? ▼
Absolutely. We love bringing workshops to schools, workplaces, faith communities, and neighborhood groups. Fill out the contact form and select "Host a workshop" to get started.
Do I need any medical background? ▼
None at all. Every program is designed for people with zero medical experience. If you can follow simple instructions, you can take one of our courses.
How do I volunteer with EEI? ▼
We're always looking for volunteers to help with events, outreach, and operations. Use the contact form below and select "Volunteer" as your reason for reaching out.
